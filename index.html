JEAN FOURCHE

Art direction branding

Jean Fourche is lauching its range of bike adapted to every member of the family and made for the city. It’s a transparent brand that shows clearly where does each components are coming from. We have established a brand inspired by the graphic codes of the retro bike clubs, and created a community open for all and all ages : humor and mutual help have their place in the Jean Fourche Cycling Club !
